Questions

What accident patients ask us

Should I see a chiropractor after a car accident?+
Many crash injuries such as whiplash and soft-tissue strain do not show up right away. A prompt evaluation lets us assess you, begin gentle care, and document your injuries properly. Call (513) 559-1200 to be seen.
Do you document injuries for insurance and attorneys?+
Yes. Proper documentation is a core part of what we do. We keep clear records of your injuries and care and coordinate with your attorney when you have one, so your case is supported by accurate clinical notes.
Do you offer transportation to appointments?+
Yes. We offer free transportation for patients who need it, so getting to your appointments after an accident is one less thing to worry about.
How do I book an appointment?+
Use the request form on this page and we will reach out to schedule you, or call us directly at (513) 559-1200. We are located at 711 E McMillan St, Cincinnati, OH 45206.
